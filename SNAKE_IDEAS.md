# Snake 100: The Ultimate Collection - Ideas List

1. **Classic**: The original experience.
2. **Speed Up**: Snake gets faster with every piece of food eaten.
3. **Slow Down**: Snake gets slower with every piece of food eaten.
4. **Big Grid**: A massive 50x50 arena.
5. **Tiny Grid**: A claustrophobic 10x10 arena.
6. **No Walls**: Wrap around the screen edges.
7. **Deadly Walls**: Touching the edge is instant game over.
8. **Moving Walls**: Obstacles that slide across the screen.
9. **Portals**: Enter one portal, exit another.
10. **Ice Floor**: Snake slides in the current direction until it hits a wall or turns.
11. **Mud Floor**: Movement speed is halved in certain zones.
12. **Ghost Mode**: Snake can pass through its own body.
13. **Shrink Food**: Special food that reduces snake length.
14. **Double Food**: Two pieces of food are always on screen.
15. **Golden Food**: Rare food that gives 10x points.
16. **Rotten Food**: Food that reduces length and points.
17. **Magnet**: Food is pulled towards the snake's head.
18. **Time Freeze**: Collect an item to stop all moving obstacles for 5 seconds.
19. **Reverse Controls**: Left is Right, Up is Down.
20. **Drunk Snake**: Randomly changes direction every few seconds.
21. **Multi-Snake**: Control two snakes simultaneously with the same keys.
22. **AI Rival**: Race against a computer-controlled snake to get the food.
23. **Shooting Snake**: Press Space to shoot projectiles that destroy obstacles.
24. **Bomb Food**: Eating food triggers an explosion that clears nearby walls.
25. **Gravity**: Snake constantly drifts towards the bottom of the screen.
26. **Wind**: A constant force pushes the snake in a specific direction.
27. **Dark Mode**: Only the area around the snake's head is visible.
28. **Flashlight**: A cone of light extends from the snake's head.
29. **Rainbow Snake**: The snake's body cycles through all colors.
30. **Growth Spurt**: Food makes the snake grow by 5 segments instead of 1.
31. **Tail Cutter**: Press a key to drop half your tail for a speed boost.
32. **Mirror Mode**: The screen is split; your movements are mirrored on the other side.
33. **Upside Down**: The entire game world is flipped vertically.
34. **Diagonal**: Snake can move in 8 directions instead of 4.
35. **Hexagonal Grid**: Movement on a hex grid.
36. **Triangular Grid**: Movement on a triangular grid.
37. **Circular Arena**: A round boundary instead of a square one.
38. **Boss Fight: Giant Food**: A massive food item that takes multiple hits to eat.
39. **Boss Fight: Laser Eye**: An eye in the center shoots lasers you must dodge.
40. **Minesweeper Snake**: Some tiles are hidden mines; food reveals safe areas.
41. **Tetris Snake**: Food items are Tetris blocks that stay on the grid as obstacles.
42. **Pac-Man Snake**: Eat "power pellets" to turn obstacles into edible ghosts.
43. **Rhythm Snake**: You can only move on the beat of the background music.
44. **Stealth Snake**: Avoid "guard" entities with vision cones.
45. **Tower Defense Snake**: Protect a core in the center while growing.
46. **Sokoban Snake**: Push blocks into targets using your body.
47. **Alchemy**: Eat Red + Blue food to create Purple (bonus points).
48. **Shop System**: Earn coins to buy upgrades like "Thicker Skin" or "Turbo".
49. **RPG Snake**: Level up stats like Speed, Length Resistance, and Luck.
50. **Skill Tree**: Unlock active abilities as you grow.
51. **Bullet Hell**: Dodge a constant stream of projectiles while eating.
52. **Shrinking Arena**: The walls slowly close in over time.
53. **Expanding Arena**: The grid grows larger as you eat.
54. **Teleporting Food**: Food changes position every 3 seconds.
55. **Mimic Food**: Some food items turn into walls right before you eat them.
56. **Shield**: Collect a shield to survive one collision.
57. **Dash**: Double-tap a direction to dash forward 3 tiles.
58. **Slow Motion**: Hold a key to slow down time (limited meter).
59. **Clone**: Create a temporary decoy that follows your path.
60. **Black Hole**: A central point that pulls the snake and food towards it.
61. **Supernova**: When you reach length 50, you explode and restart at length 5 with massive points.
62. **Constellation**: Food must be eaten in a specific numbered order.
63. **Math Snake**: Eat the food that solves the equation shown on screen.
64. **Word Snake**: Eat letters in order to spell specific words.
65. **Color Match**: You can only eat food that matches your current head color.
66. **Chameleon**: Snake changes color to match the tile it's currently on.
67. **Electric Snake**: Touching your own tail stuns you instead of game over.
68. **Fire Snake**: Leave a trail of fire that disappears after 3 seconds.
69. **Poison Trail**: Your trail becomes a permanent wall.
70. **Sticky Floor**: Some tiles slow you down significantly.
71. **Conveyor Belts**: Tiles that move the snake automatically.
72. **Moving Food**: Food actively moves away from the snake.
73. **Invisible Snake**: Only the head and the very tip of the tail are visible.
74. **Flickering Snake**: The snake flashes in and out of existence.
75. **Glitch Mode**: Random tiles swap places every 10 seconds.
76. **Low Gravity**: Turns are "floaty" and take longer to execute.
77. **High Gravity**: Turning requires more "force" (multiple key presses).
78. **Magnetic Walls**: Walls pull the snake towards them if you get close.
79. **Repelling Walls**: Walls push the snake away.
80. **Fragile Tail**: If any part of the tail is hit by a projectile, it breaks off.
81. **Regenerating Tail**: Tail segments slowly grow back if lost.
82. **Multi-Head**: Snake has a head at both ends; control which one is "active".
83. **Hydra**: If the snake hits itself, it splits into two smaller snakes.
84. **Wormhole**: Eating food teleports you to a completely different grid layout.
85. **Seasons**: Environment changes (Snow slows, Spring grows food faster).
86. **Day/Night Cycle**: Visibility changes over time.
87. **Underwater**: You have an oxygen bar that refills when you eat.
88. **Space**: No friction; you keep moving until you change direction.
89. **Volcano**: Lava slowly fills the screen from the bottom.
90. **Earthquake**: The entire grid shakes, potentially shifting positions.
91. **Zombie Snake**: Dead tail segments stay on the board as obstacles.
92. **Vampire Snake**: Length slowly decreases; you must eat to survive.
93. **Pacifist**: You cannot eat food; survive as long as possible with obstacles.
94. **Greedy**: Every food item has a 5-second timer before it disappears.
95. **Minimalist**: Goal is to eat food while staying as short as possible.
96. **Longest Snake**: Goal is to reach a specific length (e.g., 100).
97. **Speedrun**: Eat 10 pieces of food as fast as possible.
98. **Score Attack**: Get the highest score in 2 minutes.
99. **Zen Mode**: No walls, no self-collision, just relaxing growth.
100. **The Ultimate Mashup**: Every 30 seconds, the game randomly switches to a new mode.
