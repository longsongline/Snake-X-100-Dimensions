## 🌌 Welcome to the Multiverse
**Snake X: 100 Dimensions** isn't just a game; it's a journey through a century of variations. We've taken the core mechanics of the 1976 classic and expanded them into a sprawling collection of 100 unique game modes.

Each "Dimension" introduces a new twist:
- **🌀 Portal Storm:** Navigate through shifting wormholes.
- **⚛️ Quantum Entanglement:** Control two snakes that share a single soul.
- **🛡️ Shield Master:** Defend against incoming projectiles while growing.
- **🌋 Lava Floor:** The ground is literally disappearing beneath you.
- **👾 Glitch Mode:** Rules change randomly every 10 seconds.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

### 🎮 100 Unique Dimensions
From **Retro Classic** to **Boss Battles**, every mode offers a distinct challenge. You'll never play the same game twice.

### 👥 Local Multiplayer
Grab a friend and compete in the **2P Battle** mode on a single keyboard. Who will be the last snake standing?

### ⚡ Power-up System
Collect diverse items to gain temporary abilities:
- **Magnet:** Pull food towards you.
- **Shield:** Survive a single collision.
- **Slow-mo:** Manipulate time to navigate tight spots.
- **Dash:** Burst forward to escape danger.

### 🎨 Neon Aesthetics
Immerse yourself in a high-contrast, neon-lit world designed for maximum focus and visual flair.

## 🕹️ How to Play

### Controls
| Action | Player 1 | Player 2 |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Move** | `W` `A` `S` `D` | `↑` `↓` `←` `→` |
| **Dash** | `Shift` | `Space` |
| **Slow-mo** | `Q` | `Enter` |
| **Special** | `E` | `P` |

### Objective
Eat food to grow and increase your score. Avoid walls, obstacles, and your own tail. In special modes, follow the unique rules displayed on the screen.
